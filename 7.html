Kalpana Chawla

First Indian-born woman in space

Early Life

Kalpana Chawla was born in Karnal, India in 1962. She studied aeronautical engineering before moving to the USA for higher studies.

Career

She joined NASA and became an astronaut. In 1997, she flew on the Space Shuttle Columbia as a mission specialist.

Legacy

Kalpana Chawla inspired millions of students in India and around the world to dream big and aim for the stars.
